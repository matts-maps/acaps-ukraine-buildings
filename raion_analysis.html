Raion Analysis

Interactive map visualisation of the spatial distribution of recorded structural damage incidents across Ukraine, aggregated by raion (district) rather than oblast. The map is dynamically generated based on the selected assessment year and time frame filter type. Use the controls on the left to adjust the parameters and explore the data.

Raion boundaries reflect the 2020 administrative reform (136 raions). A small number of records reported at the level of a city with special status (Kyiv, Sevastopol) or a special zone (the Chornobyl exclusion zone) fall outside this raion layer and are not shown on the map, though they remain in the national totals reported elsewhere on this site.
